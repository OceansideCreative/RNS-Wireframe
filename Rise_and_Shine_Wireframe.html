Our Mission

Providing workforce training and jobs in the baking and food industries for people with barriers to employment.

Our Story

How It Started

Founders Photo

Rise and Shine was founded by Elizabeth Kinney and Pam Siemer, both former EDs of The Light House in Annapolis. They created a program combining professional baking training with workforce development.

What We Do

What We Do

Baking Training

Professional Baking Training

Students learn to bake from scratch using all-natural ingredients.

Classroom

Workforce Development

Resume building, mock interviews, soft skills training.

Mentoring

Wrap-Around Services

Case management, trauma-informed care, therapeutic programs.

Who We Serve

Individuals experiencing homelessness, veterans, those in recovery, formerly incarcerated, youth aging out of foster care, single mothers, and survivors of domestic violence.

How Our Program Works

Comprehensive workforce training built around baking, life skills, and personal growth.

Program Steps

The Journey

1

Application & Intake

Supportive intake process meeting each person where they are.

2

Professional Baking Training

Commercial baking from scratch with all-natural ingredients.

3

Workforce Development

Resume building, mock interviews, soft skills.

4

Wrap-Around Services

Case management, trauma-informed care, therapeutic programs.

5

Graduation & Employment

Skills, growth, and active support finding employment.

Skills

What Our Trainees Learn

🍪 Baking Fundamentals

Recipes, food safety, kitchen operations.

📄 Resume & Interview

Professional resumes, mock interviews.

🤝 Soft Skills

Teamwork, accountability, communication.

💼 Job Readiness

Weekly workforce development modules.

💛 Personal Growth

Trauma-informed care, coping strategies.

🎯 Goal Setting

Personalized case management and planning.
